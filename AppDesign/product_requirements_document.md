The initial release will focus on the following core functionalities, with fully approved designs available for each:

*   **Family Hub (`9e3c4605132c4830a33b3783312e8de1`)**:
    *   **Purpose:** A central dashboard providing an overview of all tracked family members.
    *   **Functionality:** Quick access to individual member profiles, summary health metrics, and possibly notifications or reminders relevant to the whole family.
*   **Member Dashboard (`8ee0fb26e7534b87873a43e2efd071c6`)**:
    *   **Purpose:** A personalized dashboard for an individual family member.
    *   **Functionality:** Displays key health metrics, recent activities, upcoming appointments, and quick links to manage their specific medical data.
*   **Medical Timeline (`5046156a148b441b9835e5a86dc8879b`)**:
    *   **Purpose:** Provides a chronological view of a member's medical history.
    *   **Functionality:** Allows users to review past records, treatments, medications, appointments, and other significant health events in an easy-to-digest timeline format.
*   **Add Record Sheet (`b77194b0a3e3455f8534b8871d8847f9`)**:
    *   **Purpose:** A dedicated interface for inputting new medical or health-related records.
    *   **Functionality:** Enables users to easily add new entries such as doctor visits, medication changes, test results, symptoms, and other relevant information.
